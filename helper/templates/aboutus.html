About This Portal


The online Etender Portal System services ltd. is a hosted solution for procurement services. The underlying philosophy of this solution is to provide a common e-business hub, for multiple buyers and sellers to come together and conduct business without compromising individual requirements and relationships among the participants. The system has been designed to accommodate organization specific document and process requirements. The solution offers value propositions for buyer organizations at different levels of I T adoption and readiness. For organizations at a nascent stage of I T adoption, the solution offers the Buyer Organization(s) the facility to create business documents on E T S.
